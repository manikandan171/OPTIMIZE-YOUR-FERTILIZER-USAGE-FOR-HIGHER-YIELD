Our Fertilizers

Epsom Salt

Helps boost the blooming phase and fruit production. Contains magnesium and sulfur, which are both very beneficial to tomatoes and bell peppers, enhancing their growth and yield.

Fish Emulsion

Provides a quick-release source of nutrients. This organic fertilizer is rich in nitrogen and is ideal for rapid plant growth and increased foliage.

Coffee Ground Fertilizer

Provides a slow-release source of nutrients. Coffee grounds add organic matter to the soil, improving its structure and providing a steady supply of nutrients.

Manure

Provides a slow-release source of nutrients. Manure enriches the soil with essential nutrients and organic matter, promoting healthy plant growth and improving soil fertility.

Grass Clippings Fertilizer

Provides a slow-release source of nutrients. Grass clippings decompose quickly and add valuable nutrients to the soil, enhancing soil health and promoting plant growth.

Bone Meal

Rich in phosphorus, bone meal supports root development and enhances flowering. It's excellent for plants requiring a boost in root and bloom growth.

Blood Meal

High in nitrogen, blood meal promotes vigorous leaf growth. Ideal for leafy vegetables and plants needing a nitrogen boost for lush foliage.

Compost

A balanced mix of organic matter, compost improves soil structure and provides a broad range of nutrients. It enhances soil fertility and supports sustainable plant growth.

Kelp Meal

Rich in micronutrients and growth hormones, kelp meal promotes healthy plant growth and stress resistance. It improves soil health and enhances nutrient uptake.

Green Manure

Planted cover crops that are tilled into the soil to add organic matter and nutrients. Green manure enhances soil fertility and structure, and helps in preventing soil erosion.

Seaweed Extract

A liquid fertilizer rich in trace minerals and growth hormones. Seaweed extract promotes root development, enhances plant immunity, and improves overall plant health.

Sulfur

Essential for protein synthesis in plants, sulfur helps improve plant growth and development. It's particularly useful in correcting sulfur deficiencies in the soil.

Wood Ash

Contains potassium and calcium carbonate, which help improve soil pH and provide essential nutrients. Wood ash is beneficial for plants that thrive in alkaline soil conditions.

Chicken Manure

A nutrient-rich organic fertilizer high in nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. Chicken manure improves soil fertility and promotes healthy, vigorous plant growth.

Composted Leaves

Decomposed leaves provide organic matter and essential nutrients. They enhance soil structure, increase moisture retention, and support overall plant health.

Alfalfa Meal

A natural fertilizer high in nitrogen and other essential nutrients. Alfalfa meal promotes strong plant growth, improves soil health, and enhances nutrient availability.

Why Sustainability Matters

Adopting sustainable practices helps ensure food security, promotes biodiversity, and preserves the environment for future generations.

Environmental Protection

Minimizing pollution and preserving natural ecosystems is crucial for maintaining the balance of the planet. Sustainable farming reduces chemical runoff into water sources and helps maintain biodiversity.

Economic Efficiency

Sustainability practices reduce the cost of inputs, helping farmers achieve better long-term profitability. Efficient use of resources leads to higher yields with lower expenses.

Healthier Communities

By reducing the use of harmful chemicals, sustainable farming practices improve the health of people and animals. Cleaner food and environment lead to better overall community health.
